Our History

The Arab Movie Database is a website that contains growing information on Arab films, actors, directors, and producers. 20,811 works have been entered, including 11,202 films and 4,923 TV series, and 18,131 users participated in the editing of the site as of 2012. It is the first Arab cinema database that allows interaction with its users, but it is copyrighted, meaning that its content and images are not freely licensed. The site is similar in nature to the Internet Movie Database.
